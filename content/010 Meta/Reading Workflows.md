# General
- There are two templates for taking notes on what you read: [[010 Meta/📐Book]] and  [[010 Meta/📐Literature]]
- They rely on the Templater, Zotero, and Highliter plugin
- You can incorporate #Key/example tags into your work by using Zotero's tag function or writing them into the comment itself. Zotero is preferred. 
# Structure
There are three broad tiers of structure 
- # Parsecs
	- This is the broadest possible view. For sweeping *summaries, generalizations, and main points*. 
- # Meters
	- An intermediate view for trying to contain some specificity in a lightly condensed form. Notes are done in sentence form on *chapters, characters, and themes*.
- # Plancks
	- The most granular view. Atomic, bulleted comments on the work line-by-line.
