# Welcome
Digital Garden, Second Brain, personal knowledge management... whichever name you use here it is! 

If you're curious about this concept you can learn about it more deeply elsewhere. Suffice to say this little site allows me to share my Obsidian notes. 

So welcome to my brain. Or at least a heavily lobotomized of it version. 

In addition to the frustrating amount of thinking I fail to or cannot record, substantial portions of it are redacted for the privacy of others and myself. If you chance upon a deadlink, just imagine whatever kind of dirty laundry you please!

# How to navigate 
I see there are two slightly conflicting uses for this. Choose your path, after all they say it's about the journey right?
#### The Interested Observer
That's you, assuming you haven't lost interest already. Providing a intriguing, exploratory way to peruse my thoughts [[010 Meta/Curated Portal🔹🔸\|might look more like playing Portal]].  
#### The Productivity Monkey
That's me. I have some real use cases to access and categorize information quickly. I know what I need to find, or at least where to look. This ends up looking more like [[000/000 Guide\|the brutally practical but unsexy yellow pages]].
