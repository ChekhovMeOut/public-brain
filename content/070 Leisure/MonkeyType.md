> [!introduction]
> - Is a [free website](https://monkeytype.com/) I've been using to improve my general typing speed. 
> - It was recommended to me by [[060 People/Aidan Capaldi]]. 
> - BTW the 'English' vocabulary is the top 200 words.

> [!methodology]
> - It is possible to do many different variants of the speed test in MonkeyType. For variety's sake, it's good to mix and match. 
> - However, for the purposes of actually monitoring the trajectory of improvement there should be standardized tests that I compare myself against.
> 
> ### Time-based
> - English 1K for 30s + punctuation
> - English 10K for 30s + punctuation
> - English 10K for 30s + punctuation + numbers
