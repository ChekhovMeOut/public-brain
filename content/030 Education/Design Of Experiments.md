> [!definition]- Integrated Effects Testing (IET)
> Testing the entire reactor system with all the parts, except for fissile material. Electric heaters are used instead. 

> [!definition]- Separate Effects Testing
> Individual components or systems are assembled and tested. Examples are steam condensation studies, turbine performance, reactor core thermal hydraulics for nucleation and heat flux, pump flow curve validation, material irradiation under typical conditions
